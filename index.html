Mr. Kim_Chi 64

How to play

Like most platform games, all you need to do is:

  1. Jump: Press the up-arrow key and you will see Mr. Kim_Chi jump
  2. Go Right Press the right-arrow key and Mr. Kim_Chi will face right and move
  3. Go Left Press the left-arrow key and Mr. Kim_Chi will change direction
